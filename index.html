CITY OF IMUS SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM

Educational Assistance for the students of Imus City.

ABOUT THE PROGRAM

Welcome to the City of Imus Scholarship Program, A tribute of Mayor Alex Advincula's consistent commitment to education and developing the community.

This program aims to empower the youth of Imus by giving them access to high-quality educational opportunities, all under the visionary leadership of Mayor Advincula.

The scholarship program supports deserving students in following their academic goals, regardless of their background or financial condition, with a focus on diversity and merit.

Mayor Advincula partners with local stakeholders and educational institutions to make sure the initiative keeps growing and has a significant impact on the lives of Imus people. Under the unwavering direction of Mayor Alex Advincula, join us in our mission to use education to foster a better future for the City of Imus.
